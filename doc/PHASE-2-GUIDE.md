# VaultFlow - Phase 2: Operations Surface and Real-Time UX

## Overview
**Duration:** 2 weeks  
**Goal:** Build the day-to-day operating surface for invoices, clients, dashboards, charts, tables, and workflow visibility.

## Sprint Breakdown

### Sprint 2.1 - Data Model and Dashboard Foundations
- Invoice and client schema
- Dashboard KPI cards and chart components
- Revenue and status visualizations
- Role-aware dashboard shell

### Sprint 2.2 - Invoice Workspace
- Invoice table with search, sorting, and pagination
- Invoice detail lifecycle
- Draft, sent, viewed, paid, overdue, and cancelled states
- Line items, totals, and workflow actions

### Sprint 2.3 - Client Workspace and Realtime
- Client directory and client detail views
- Realtime invoice and activity updates
- Notifications and operational context
- Empty states and workflow guidance

### Sprint 2.4 - Reporting and Experience Polish
- Reporting views and analytics snapshots
- SSR/ISR strategy where appropriate
- Loading skeletons and resilient states
- Consistent component behavior across dashboard surfaces

## Expected Output

- Operators can manage invoices and clients from a credible product workspace
- Finance and reporting views feel cohesive instead of demo-only
- Realtime updates and workflow context improve operational continuity
